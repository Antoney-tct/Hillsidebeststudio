About Us

Our Story

Born from a Love
of Light

The Hillside Studios started in 2016 with a single camera, a pickup truck, and an obsession with finding the perfect light. What began as weekend portrait sessions in the hills of Ruiru grew into one of Kenya's most sought-after photography and videography studios.

We believe deeply that great photography is not just about technical skill — it's about building trust with the people in front of your lens, so they can be fully themselves. That philosophy runs through every project we take on.

Today, our team covers weddings, brand campaigns, events, and cinematic films across Kenya and beyond. The hills are still our home. The light is still our obsession.
