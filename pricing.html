Frequently Asked Questions

How do I secure my booking date? +

A 30% non-refundable deposit is required to hold your date. The balance is due 7 days before the shoot.

Do you travel outside Nairobi? +

Yes! We cover all of Kenya and travel internationally by arrangement. Travel costs are quoted separately based on location.

What happens if it rains on our wedding day? +

Rain makes for the most cinematic images. We come prepared and adapt. Rescheduling is available for extreme weather with 48 hours notice.

How many images will we receive? +

Packages outline minimums — in practice we always over-deliver. A full wedding day typically yields 400–600 finished images.

Can we request specific shots? +

Absolutely. We'll go through a detailed shot list in your pre-shoot consultation to make sure nothing is missed.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes — for packages above KES 75,000 we can split the balance into two payments. Contact us to arrange.
